General

What is Vama?

▾

Vama is a cross-platform messaging app with a built-in AI agent. Chat, call, share media, and manage tasks — all in one app. Available on iOS, Android, Mac, Windows, and web.

Is Vama free?

▾

Yes. Messaging, calls, and the basic AI agent (powered by Google Gemini) are free. Premium AI models — Claude Sonnet and Claude Opus — come with $5 in free credit. After that, pay only for what you use with no subscription required.

What platforms is Vama available on?

▾

Vama is available on iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, and any web browser. Your account syncs seamlessly across all devices.

How do I get started?

▾

Download Vama from the App Store, Google Play, or vama.com. Sign up with your phone number — no email required. Your AI agent activates in one tap.

AI Agent

What can the AI agent do?

▾

Your agent can manage email, research anything, draft messages, book appointments, automate tasks, and connect to 150+ apps. It has access to 40,000+ skills — just ask naturally.

Which AI models are available?

▾

Vama supports Google Gemini (always free), Claude Sonnet, and Claude Opus. Claude models include $5 in free credit to start — after that, pay per use with no subscription. Switch models anytime in settings.

Can I have multiple agents?

▾

Yes. You can create multiple AI agents in Vama, each as its own contact. Assign different agents to different jobs — work, personal, research, and more.

Is my conversation with the agent private?

▾

Yes. Agent conversations are private and not used to train AI models. We do not share your data with third parties.

Messaging

Is Vama end-to-end encrypted?

▾

Yes. All messages and calls are end-to-end encrypted by default. Only you and your recipients can read your conversations.

Can I make group calls?

▾

Yes. Vama supports group voice and video calls with up to 32 participants. Screen sharing is available during any call.

Does Vama support disappearing messages?

▾

Yes. You can set any conversation to auto-delete messages after a time period of your choosing.

How do I switch to a new phone?

▾

Sign in with your phone number on your new device and your account — messages and settings — restores automatically. No manual backup needed.

Account & Privacy

How do I change my phone number?

▾

Go to Settings → Account → Phone Number. You'll need access to your current number to verify the change before switching.

How do I delete my account?

▾

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent. You have a 45-day window to restore your account after requesting deletion.

What happens when I delete my account?

▾

Your profile, messages, and data are permanently deleted. You'll be removed from all groups. Content shared in conversations may remain visible to others.

Does Vama sell my data?

▾

No. Vama does not sell personal data or scan messages for advertising. Privacy is a core part of how we build the product.
